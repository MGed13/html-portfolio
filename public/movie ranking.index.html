The Best Movies According To Murray

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Inglorious Bastards

Such a great movie from start to finish. Some fantastic acting from the cast and can watch over and over again.

He-Man Masters Of The Universe

My favourite movie growing up. Used to watch this with my older sister and know the script word for word. Classic cheesy acting however it has to go down as my top 3 movies of all time.

Home Alone

Favourite Christmas movie of all time and can watch this without it being Christmas. Classic favourite and also another movie I know the script word for word.
